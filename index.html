Categories and Rules

Parade

The routine in this category is to not exceed 6 minutes. This category is for your team to showcase what they do best. In this category the judges will look for precision, stunts, use of floor, formations, synchronization, showmanship, difficulty, and originality.

Creative Dance

The routine in this category is to not exceed 6 minutes. This category is for your team to showcase what they do best. In this category the judges will look for precision, stunts, use of floor, formations, synchronization, showmanship, difficulty, and originality.

Stand Battle

The routine in this category is to not exceed 6 minutes. This category is for your team to showcase what they do best. In this category the judges will look for precision, stunts, use of floor, formations, synchronization, showmanship, difficulty, and originality.

Rules and Info

The Queen of City Competition will start promptly at 2pm. Check-In time is at 10:00am.

1. The team will have 5 to 7 min to execute a routine. Timing will begin when the 1st movement starts. Points will be deduction for each minute over.

2. Clean Music on a CD or Thumb Drive! (Please pack an extra copy for back up) Label you CD with Division, team name, number and category.

3. All coaches and instructors are not allowed on the floor at any time while their team is performing.

4. Please don’t stand behind the judges table while your team is performing.

5. Coaches please allow your team from walking during another team performance. Please respect each team. Excessive walking will result in deduction from final points.

6. All uniforms must be appropriate!

7. Footwear is recommended.

8. Pictures will be taken after each team performance.

9. All teams must arrive by 10:00am no later than 12:00pm. Each team will be given 10 min to run through their routine and to ensure music is functioning properly. The doors will be open at 12:00pm, so please get there early.

10. All teams must be respectful to others!

11. Have FUN!

12. All team member names must be on roll to enter into competition. If the child name is not on roll they will pay $10.00 to enter.

13. All team members must enter with their director at the back door of Blount High School. No child will enter after directors sign in. Parents and other guest will enter in front of building.

14. Please park buses at the on Side parking area of Blount High School.

15. Any team that does not adhere to the terms and procedures of these Rules and Regulations will be subject to disqualification. Each team in each category must have a registration form.

>16. Trophies will be given out at the end of the program.

17. Only two sponsors will be allowed free pass to enter event for free.

18. Teammates that are not performing, but are present must pay to attend event.

19. Advance Tickets are $10.00. Tickets will go on sale April 6, 2019. The Admission at door is $12.00.

20. If any director or sponsor need advanced tickets for parents, please fill out form in packet with collect amount and I will mail tickets back to you.

21.ALL payments are final NO REFUNDS!!!!! There will be a $50.00 Registration fee

21. Only two tickets will be given to team for Hospitality Room.

22. NO OUTSIDE FOOD OR DRINKS ARE AVAIABLE!!!
